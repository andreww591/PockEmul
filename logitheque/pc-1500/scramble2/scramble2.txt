CLOAD "SCRAMBLE 2"

1st execution:
RUN
Next times:
DEF A or RUN

Game :
(Game in French) Go as far as you can. Get more fuel by shooting isolated dots 1 row from the top or the bottom of the screen.

Keys :
(4)(0) - up/down
(8)(9)(/) - stop/normal/fast
(E) horizontal shoot
(Z) vertical shoot
